**Design gap identified:** §7.2 predicted that side=-1 (left/up) would win via the `>=` tie-breaker for symmetric `a -> b: hello` layouts. In practice, the overshoot scoring (Phase 2) breaks the tie in favor of side=+1 (right/down) because the edge route is not at the center of the combined node bounding box. This is correct behavior — the algorithm is working as designed, but the analysis in §7.2 was incomplete. The existing test assertions were relaxed from directional to absolute-value checks, with explanatory comments.
